About Us

  • Our Goals

    The main goal of our project is to help student to find a free room in Unitn's buildings that they can use to study (when other study rooms are full) or to do group projects.

  • -Choose a building to discover actual free rooms.
  • -Choose a building to discover free rooms for future days.
  • -Choose a room to discover its schedule.
  • -Use our dynamic map to easily find a free rooms for your studies.
  • -All these features will be available on Telegram! bot: @bot
